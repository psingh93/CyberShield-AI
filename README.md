## ✨ Key Capabilities & Features

### 1. Zero-Trust Phishing & URL Parser
- Automatically evaluates suspicious emails, SMS headers, and raw URLs.
- Identifies social engineering tactics, look-alike domains (typosquatting), and hidden tracking parameters.

### 2. Live Threat Intelligence Search
- Leverages intelligent orchestration to fetch current, zero-day threat feeds and database definitions dynamically.
- Translates dynamic cyber intelligence indicators into instant safety scoring.

### 3. Human-Readable Risk Mitigation
- Breaks down highly technical attack patterns (e.g., ransomware, cross-site scripting) into plain language.
- Generates immediate, step-by-step containment checklists for impacted users.
